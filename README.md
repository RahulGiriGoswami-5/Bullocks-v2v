# 📖 Overview

Sahayika is a smart women safety platform designed to provide quick and reliable assistance during emergencies. The platform combines community reporting, safe check-ins, trusted contacts, live safety maps, and emergency tools into one seamless experience.

The goal is to ensure users can seek help, notify trusted people, and make informed decisions while traveling.
